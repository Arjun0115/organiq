Beetroot juice Benifits:-

Beetroot and its juice help your heart and lungs work better during exercise.
Nitric oxide from beets increases blood flow to your muscles.
Beets are rich in folate (vitamin B9), which helps cells grow and function .

Beetroot juice 100 ml natural contains:

  1. Calories: Approximately 43 kcal, making it a relatively low-calorie drink
  2. Protein: Around 1 gram per 100 ml.
  3. Vitamin C: Acts as an antioxidant, supporting immune function and skin health.
  4. Folate (Vitamin B9): Important for cell growth and heart health
  5. Potassium: Helps regulate blood pressure and fluid balance in the body.
  6. Carbohydrates: Roughly 9.5 grams, with natural sugars making up the majority
  7. Iron: Beet juice contains some iron, which is essential for red blood cell production.
